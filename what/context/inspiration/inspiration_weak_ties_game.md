# Inspiration: Weak Ties Graph Traversal Game

> **Status**: Adjacent inspiration shared by user during founding architecture session. **NOT a canonical design constraint for MOP.** See `AGENTS.md` for usage rules.

## Source

Shared by the operator on 2026-04-24, in conversation with the agent during MOP architecture resolution.

## Summary

A structured Milgram-style six-degrees experiment, framed as a competitive/cooperative game. Players attempt to reach one of 9 high-leverage targets (CEOs, heads of state) through voice-relay only, propagating through trust networks. Rules require:

- Voice-call-only propagation (the user is explicitly suspicious of *written* social media as a category)
- Each hop must agree to play *before* details are shared (commit-before-disclose mechanic)
- Adjacency-only knowledge (no inquiring about non-adjacent participants)
- Time limit: May 15, 2026
- Prizes: speed bounty + group bounty + knowledge prize

Philosophical conjecture: high-trust society is collapsing because information fails to permeate across clusters (sub-percolation), driving cancerous homogeneity and mimetic conflict. Restoring weak-tie traversal might restore something.

## Why this informs MOP (without constraining it)

| Substrate from Weak Ties Game | What it suggests for MOP |
|-------------------------------|--------------------------|
| Trust-mediated, voice-relay distribution | MOP's "sharing happens over existing rails, not a feed" thesis depends on the same percolation dynamics |
| Commit-before-disclose mechanic | Same family as bearer capabilities (possession implies access); both restrict information flow to knowing-recipients |
| Adjacency-only knowledge | Aligns with MOP's host-blind property (no global view, no chain visibility, no graph) |
| Network-percolation framing | The user thinks natively in network/percolation terms — important calibration for any future scope decision that touches "discovery" or "graph" |

## Where it does NOT apply to MOP

- MOP is a **text** medium. The user's suspicion of written social media is a category-critique, not an instruction to build a voice product.
- MOP has no game/competition primitive.
- MOP has no targets list or time-limited prize structure.
- The user explicitly framed this as orthogonal/inspiration, not specification.
